Priya Shahi

Namaste, my name is Priya and I am from Queens, New York -- one of the most diverse places in the country. I grew up being exposed to many other cultures, and along the way I learned how to dance salsa from my Dominican neighbors, how to braid hair from my Malian friend in high school, and I grew to love Thai food after a bubble tea spot opened up a few blocks away from my apartment. My exposure to my own South Asian culture and other ethnicities drew me to get my BA in Journalism to become a storyteller. We are all differnet and it is through understanding one another that we can become more compassionate in this world.

I am currently a reporter covering community driven stories, including local politics, education, and public health in New York City through writing, photography, and videography.
